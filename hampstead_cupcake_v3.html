Come to Hampstead Parochial School to create your own customised cupcake!!!

At The Custom Cake, our fantastic cupcake stand, we will give you the opportunity of customising your own cupcake to perfection.

The very fun mission

Turn a plain cupcake into a masterpiece

Your mission is to turn a dull, plain cupcake into a masterpiece like the one in the left corner by using flavorful treats, amazing add-ons, and flavorsome chocolate sauce.

Delicious Celebration Awaits!

Craft it. Then eat it.

Once you've crafted your amazing masterpiece, it's time for the best part — eating it! Gather around with your friends and family to share in the deliciousness of your creation. With every bite, you'll celebrate your incredible accomplishment and the fun you had making it. Let the joy and laughter fill the air as you enjoy your tasty treat. This experience will give you precious moments with your loved ones.
